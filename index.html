Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Click the "Sign Up" button, enter your details, and choose whether you're a customer or professional. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.
How do I update my payment method?
Go to Settings → Billing Information → Payment Methods to add, edit, or remove payment methods. All payments are processed securely.
Can I delete my account?
Yes, you can delete your account in Settings → Danger Zone. This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed.
How do I book a service?
Search for the service you need, browse professional profiles, read reviews, and click "Book Now" on your preferred professional. You can schedule at a time that works for you.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule?
You can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment without penalty. Go to your booking history and select the appropriate option.
Are all professionals verified?
Yes, all professionals undergo background checks, license verification, and insurance validation before joining our platform. Your safety is our priority.
How do I become a professional on QuickFix Pro?
Sign up with a professional account, complete your profile, upload licenses and insurance documents, and pass our verification process. You can start receiving bookings once approved.
What fees does QuickFix Pro charge?
We charge a small commission fee of 1.5% on completed jobs. There are no monthly fees or hidden charges. You keep the majority of what you earn.
How do I get paid?
Payments are processed automatically after job completion. Funds are available for withdrawal within 24-48 hours. You can withdraw to your bank account or keep earnings in your QuickFix wallet.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Account Issues

Can't log in to my account

Try resetting your password, check your email for verification, or clear your browser cache.

Email verification not received

Check spam folder, ensure correct email address, or request a new verification email.

Profile information won't save

Ensure all required fields are filled, check internet connection, and try refreshing the page.

Booking Problems

Can't find available professionals

Try expanding your search radius, checking different time slots, or browsing different service categories.

Booking confirmation not received

Check your email and SMS notifications, verify contact information in your profile.

Professional didn't show up

Contact support immediately for assistance and potential refund or rebooking.

Payment Issues

Payment failed or declined

Verify card details, check account balance, or try a different payment method.

Refund not received

Refunds typically take 3-5 business days. Check with your bank or contact support if delayed.

Incorrect charge amount

Review booking details and contact support with transaction ID for investigation.

Technical Problems

App or website running slowly

Clear browser cache, check internet connection, or try using a different browser.

Features not working properly

Try refreshing the page, disable browser extensions, or contact technical support.

Mobile app crashes

Update to latest version, restart your device, or reinstall the app.
